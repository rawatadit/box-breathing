What is Box Breathing?

Box breathing, also known as square breathing, is a powerful stress-relief technique used by Navy SEALs, athletes, and healthcare professionals. It's called "box" breathing because each phase takes the same amount of time, creating a square or box pattern.

Inhale - 4 seconds
Hold - 4 sec
Hold - 4 sec
Exhale - 4 seconds

How to Practice Effectively

1

Find Your Space

Sit comfortably in a quiet place. You can close your eyes or keep them softly focused.

2

Start Small

Begin with 4-5 cycles (about 1-2 minutes). Gradually increase as you get comfortable.

3

Be Consistent

Practice daily, even just for 2 minutes. Consistency matters more than duration.

4

Don't Force It

If 4 seconds feels too long, start with 3. The rhythm matters more than the exact timing.
